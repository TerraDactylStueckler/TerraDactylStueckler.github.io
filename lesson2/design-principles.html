alison.com

Alison has a nice use of alignment, with all their topics equidistant from each other with the side margins also being equal. The text on the home page is nicely aligned center & seems to draw the eye toward the courses below.

duolingo.com/register

Duolingo uses repetion in the fonts throughout their site & also in the way they present the different languages they offer. The mini-cards for each language present the same size & each shows the number of subscribers for each language.

wuspba.com

WUSPBA has a very good use of contrast. The white text against a dark background is very clear & easy to read. The button to take you to events is very prominent with it's different color. The logo also contrasts very well with the background.
