About the Classes

Although these are "gun classes", the emphasis is on YOU, not your gun. You are your best weapon, and your everything else is just an extension of that. We will train your body and mind to react to danger efficiently and effectively regardless of age and physical condition. Learn how to make the most of what you have!

Regardless of whether you have a handgun, rifle, shotgun or no firearm for protection, these classes will build your knowledge and skills. They are a blend of the NRA Pistol Shooting curriculum (so you can get your concealed carry permit if you choose) and field proven Lander Defense curriculum for all firearms and empty hands.

These classes will not be too advanced for you. They are designed for anyone with little or no knowledge of self defense and firearms. After completing the three Novice Classes, you will know:

  • Basic danger avoidance skills
  • How to start thinking like a defender, not a victim
  • How to operate and shoot your defensive firearm safely
  • What to expect while learning self defense
  • How to choose the right firearm(s) for you
