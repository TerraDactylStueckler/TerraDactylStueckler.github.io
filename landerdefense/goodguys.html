About the Classes

Being a "good guy with a gun" is 98% good guy and 2% gun. You are your best weapon, and everything else is just an extension of that. Responding to a deadly threat can leave you injured or in trouble with the law if you have not had the right training. Let us build on what you may already know, to become a better protector of what you love.

Regardless of whether you have a handgun, rifle, shotgun or no firearm for protection, these classes will build your knowledge and skills. They are a blend of mindset, awareness, firearms and empty hands methods.

These classes will start at ground level and build on your existing knowledge and experiences. It will start with a solid foundation in safety and gun handling, and progress you to a high level skill set. Wether you are a beginner or experienced, you will be challenged by the curriculum. After completing the first three classes, you will know:

  1. Danger avoidance skills
  2. Thinking like a defender
  3. Defense appropriate operation of your defensive firearm safely
  4. What to reasonably expect while learning self defense.
  5. How to choose the right firearm(s) and gear for you.
  6. How to advance your training to achieve your goals.
