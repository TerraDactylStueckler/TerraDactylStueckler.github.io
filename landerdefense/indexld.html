After the Aurora Colorado Theater shooting, it became obvious that the gunman could have been easily stopped if only a very few people in the crowd had a real fighting mindset. Not just being a “Good Guy with a Gun”, but having a deep understanding formed from physical experience about what would work and what wouldn’t. Knowing what to do in a situation like that doesn’t happen on your first time through, but there simply wasn’t any way available to train for it ahead of time, and that needed to change. There needed to be a real fighting school for civilians, so I built the Lander Defense Team and started running classes.

Mama Bear Classes

Learn safety and confidence with firearms along with mindset and situational awareness. Firearm and non-firearm defense is taught with the law and legality of self defense. Earn your concealed carry permit certification. Each class is offered in three convenient time formats: Two sessions, each 1.5 hours long, in the evenings on two nights. One session on a Saturday morning for 3 hours. One full 8 hour day covering Novice 1 - Novice 3 Classes are between 1-2 months apart. Homework that can be done in about 10 minutes a day.

Good Guys with Guns

You know who you are. You can't stand evil people victimizing the innocent. You are not a policeman. You are not a soldier. You want to be able to make a difference if you are needed. Maybe you know how to shoot and already carry a gun, maybe you don't. Maybe you have been trained, maybe you have not. You probably already know that you need to improve on some things. Lander Defense is for people like you. The Good Guys With Guns program is not a one size fits all "class". It is an individual coaching program designed to reinforce your skills and reveal your weak points so you can work on them.
