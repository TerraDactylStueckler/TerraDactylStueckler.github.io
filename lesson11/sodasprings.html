Soda Springs is a veritable source of beauty and wonder. The surrounding countryside is full of opportunity to enjoy nature in all its bounty & wonder. Travelers to this area are able to enjoy the fresh air as they stroll along mountain paths. The variety of wildlife is astounding & quite often the contemplative hiker can happen upon an idyllic scene of deer drinking from a quietly burbling stream. Accomodations for these hearty adventurerers can be found in our quaint town complete with all the amenities. Come enjoy Soda Springs!
