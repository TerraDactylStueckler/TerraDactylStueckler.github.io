Audience

The audience for this site is people who are interested in being prepared for the weather at upcoming events in this area, simply and easily accessible through a well designed web site.

Persona 1:

A mother with young children at home who is wanting to take them to the park for some well deserved outdoor time.

Persona 2:

A family planning committee.

Scenario 1:

A family in the area is hosting a family reunion. They need to know the weather for the potential activities so the rest of the family can be prepared for the weather eventualities.

Scenario 2:

A young mother wants to take her children to the park. She would like to spend as much time as possible and have her children enjoy the experience so that it can be repeated in the future.

Scenario 3:

An older couple wants to attend a popular community event and needs to know if they need some form of light coat or what appropriate sun shade to bring.

Scenario 4:

A family is going camping and needs to prepare the proper gear and food supplies to bring. They want their children to enjoy this so that it can become a yearly event.

Scenario 5:

Out of town visitors to the area would like to know the weather and proper attire for their trip.
